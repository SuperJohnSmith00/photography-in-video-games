Retro Video Game Photos

Previous Next

This is where you'll find all of the retro video game photos I take! Unfortunately as retro games become harder and harder to encounter, I will only update this page when I have a photo for it. Now of course, capturing decades old pixels can be quite challenging! But not to worry, I have a great source for them! Although, one thing I will say though is the definition of retro is a rather loose term. It could refer to the gamecube and original Xbox or it could refer to the NES and Sega Genesis. The further and further we get from the early stages of video games, the more recent things will become retro and so on and so forth. In a few years from now, the most powerful PCs and consoles will be nothing more than scrap metal, extra parts, and maybe even relics. The future can never be predicted, but we can enjoy it all the more.
