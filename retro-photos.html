Retro Video Game Photos

This is where you'll find all the retro video game photos I take! Unfortunately, as retro games become increasingly difficult to find, I will only update this page when a photo is available for it. It will most likely end up in the carousel rather than being individually added below this. Now, capturing decades-old pixels can be quite challenging, of course. But not to worry, I have a source for them. One thing I will say is that the definition of retro is a rather loose term and can be difficult to define. It could refer to the GameCube and original Xbox, or it could refer to the NES and Sega Genesis. The further we get from the early stages of video games, the more recent things will become retro. In a few years from now, the most powerful PCs and consoles will be nothing more than scrap metal, extra parts, and maybe even relics. The future can never be predicted, but we can relish it all the more.
