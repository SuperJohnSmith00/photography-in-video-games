Here are some non-video game photos I've taken!

My name is John Smith or SuperJohnSmith00 or just John. I decided to make this website about photography and video games because those are 2 of my largest hobbies of all time. Photography has always interested me more than the average Joe. Same with video games. Why not combine the 2? It's like chocolate and peanut butter. Or caramel, if you're like me. This website is purely a middle-of-the-night thought turned into a project.
