Who am I?

My name is John Smith/SuperJohnSmith00, or just John. I decided to make this website about photography and video games because those are 2 of my largest hobbies of all time. Photography has always interested me more than the average Joe. Same with video games. Why not combine the 2? It's like chocolate and peanut butter. Or caramel, if you're like me. This website is purely a middle-of-the-night thought turned into a project. In the carousel, there are images that are related to some of my favorite games. The song on this page is The Promised Consort from Elden Ring.
