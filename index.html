New Home*

*This is a reference to the final area of Undertale, also named "New Home"

Photography is as old as time itself, while video games are only around 67 years old (as of 2025). Combining the two is now easier than ever before with the help of built-in screenshotting through consoles and PC platforms and in-game photo modes! It's like what Gusteau from Ratatouille said, "Anyone can cook!" but instead of food, it's photographing digital landscapes and such. Feel free to explore the pages through the navbar at the top of the screen! Most, if not all, the pages will be updated weekly with new info, images, videos, and more!
