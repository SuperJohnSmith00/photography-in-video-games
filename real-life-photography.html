Real Life Photography

Video game photography doesn't just mean photos of games themselves through screenshots, recording software, and taking images of the screen itself. Video game photography is a sort of umbrella term for a lot of smaller, more intricate topics. In this case, IRL video game photography is taking photos of gaming events, activities, or even just you and a few friends playing something together. The song on this page is CORE by Toby Fox.

This is the Steam Deck, a handheld PC made by the company Valve made specifically for playing PC games anywhere you go, similar to a Nintendo Switch, ROG Ally, Lenovo Legion Go, and other similar gaming handhelds.




Example 2

Example 3
