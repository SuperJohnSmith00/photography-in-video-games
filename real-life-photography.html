Real Life Photography

Video game photography doesn't just mean photos of games themselves through screenshots, recording softwares, and taking images of the screen itself. Video game photography is a sort of umbrella term for a lot of smaller, more intricate topics. In this case, IRL video game photography is taking photos of gaming events, activities, or even just you and a few friends playing something together.
