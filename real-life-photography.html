IRL (In Real Life) Video Game Photography

Previous Next

Above are some images I took at CHS' Esports club. They were used in a phamphlet advertising their new academy!

Video game photography doesn't just mean photos of games themselves through screenshots, recording softwares, and taking images of the screen itself. Video game photography is a sort of umbrella term for a lot of more smaller topics. In this case, IRL video game photography is taking photos of gaming events, activities, or even just you and a few friends playing something together. Now of course, "real life" is a rather large term so there may be a non-video game related photo every once and a while until I find/take a photo that suits my standards for this website.
