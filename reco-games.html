March Game of the Month

HELLDIVERS 2

A quick description from the developers (can be found on the Steam page): "HELLDIVERS™ 2 is a 3rd person squad-based shooter that sees the elite forces of the Helldivers battling to win an intergalactic struggle to rid the galaxy of the rising alien threats. From a 3rd person perspective, players use a variety of weapons (pistols, machine guns, flamethrowers) and stratagems (turrets, airstrikes, etc.) to shoot and kill the alien threats." While you may play as the bad guy lore-wise, that doesn't mean the gameplay sucks. In fact, it's a gold mine of potential which is unfortunately wasted by a seemingly incompetent dev team that relies on external studios for most, if not all, of the content released in recent times. While outsourcing is an industry norm, it leaves the question of "What does Arrowhead do?" This question has been left unanswered to this day.
