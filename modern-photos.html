Modern video game photos are a variety grab-bag nowadays. One moment you'll see an absolutely stunning Los Santos sunset, only to be hit by a screenshot of a Fortnite bug or an accidental Clash Royale screenshot. Photography may have a lot of regulations and rules, but many of them don't apple to this type of photography. Why? Because this can be whatever you want it to be. Through the usage of filters, editing software, and 3D modeling software, addons, mods, and whatever else, you can make anything come alive! Also, this is where you'll find all of the modern video game photos I take! There will be at least 1 or 2 a week as well as a small description underneath. Maybe more, maybe less. Who knows! Videography and Photography work hand in hand with modern day video games. Take the Daft Punk Experience in Fortnite for example, it let people live through a concert they most likely missed and lets them record it and meet like-minded people! What's not to love?
