Modern Video Game Photos

Modern video game photos are a grab-bag nowadays. One moment, you'll see an absolutely stunning Los Santos sunset, only to see an absolute abomination made in Minecraft. Photography may have numerous regulations and rules, but many of them don't apply to this type of photography. Why? Because this can be whatever you want it to be. Through the usage of filters, editing software, and 3D modeling software, addons, mods, and whatever else, you can make anything come alive! Additionally, this is where you'll find all the modern video game photos I've taken. There will be at least one image a week. Maybe more, maybe less. Who knows! You may notice that images are very low quality, but don't fret! Simply click (or tap) on them and you'll be sent to the full resolution image! The song on this page is World's End Rhapsody by Nujabes.
